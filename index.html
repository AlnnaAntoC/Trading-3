Mission

Why We Exist

Chalakkal Group was created to solve a problem that most logistics companies avoided — moving heavy, complex, and high-value industrial cargo efficiently and reliably. We started in 2000 with a fleet of 20 flatbed trucks and a contract to move oilfield equipment across the Gulf.

Today we've grown into a full-spectrum logistics provider, but that industrial DNA runs through everything we do. We're the team called when the cargo is difficult, the deadline is tight, and the stakes are high.

kochi Operations
Vision

Building the Future

We're investing in the next generation of industrial logistics: hydrogen-powered HGVs, autonomous warehouse systems, and AI-driven route planning that adapts in real time to traffic, weather, and border conditions.

Our target: 40% emissions reduction by 2030 through a combination of fleet electrification, renewable-energy warehouses, and carbon-offset partnerships.
